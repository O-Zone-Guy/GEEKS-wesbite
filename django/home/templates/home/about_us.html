We run events and services for all interests that include but are not limited to gaming, tabletop games, anime, and beyond. As we head into an online school semester, we are committed to nurturing our virtual space where students can participate in club events and build a community that spans out beyond Hamilton. Our Discord is where our online events are hosted, open for everyone to join, and a place to share anything geeky!
